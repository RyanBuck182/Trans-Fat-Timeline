Trans Fat Timeline

Trans fats have been part of the American diet for many decades. Over time, research has raised concerns about the safety of trans fats for human consumption. Today, the primary source of trans fats has effectively been banned by the FDA. This timeline will provide an overview of the development of artificial trans fats all the way up to their removal from the American diet.
